# Image Mapping
Image mapping is used to bulk edit images in your elements.

To open the mapping tool, click *Open Image Mapping Widget* inside of the *Image Mapping* section. You should see something like this:

![](../static/image-mapping-widget.gif)

The tool is super simple! Just apply all image IDs to their respective instances, and when finished, click "apply all images".
